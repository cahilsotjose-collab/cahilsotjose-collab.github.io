What I Offer

I offer a variety of Salesforce QA and Development services to help you optimize your Salesforce systems, automate business processes, and deliver high-quality solutions. Here are the key services I provide:

1. QA Buddy & Manual Testing Support (Light Involvement)

Approximate Hours per Week: 5-10 hours

Perfect for companies needing on-demand support for manual testing, this option is flexible and requires minimal commitment. I step in when extra hands are needed or when you need expert testing on specific areas.

  • Manual Test Case Creation & Execution: I’ll create and execute manual test cases based on your project’s acceptance criteria.
  • Regression Testing: Conduct thorough manual regression testing to ensure no new bugs are introduced in existing features.
  • Bug Tracking & Reporting: Track and report defects using JIRA or your preferred tool, ensuring issues are quickly addressed.
  • On-Demand QA Support: I’m available for short-term or ongoing assistance based on your testing needs.

2. Salesforce QA & Custom Configuration (Medium Involvement)

Approximate Hours per Week: 10-20 hours

Ideal for companies that need more structured manual testing and Salesforce configuration support. This option provides a solid balance of testing and configuration tasks, allowing your Salesforce system to run smoothly while I help streamline key processes.

  • Salesforce QA Testing: Perform manual QA testing across Salesforce environments.
  • Salesforce Custom Configuration: Configure Salesforce objects, flows, and CPQ to meet specific business needs.
  • Regression Test Suites: Execute manual regression test suites and validate core functionality during each release.
  • Process Optimization: Help improve Salesforce workflows through manual testing and system validation to enhance efficiency.

3. Full-Scale QA & Salesforce Solutions (High Involvement)

Approximate Hours per Week: 20-40 hours

For companies requiring dedicated and comprehensive manual testing alongside Salesforce development and support. This option provides the most involvement, with me fully integrated into your testing and Salesforce solution workflows.

  • Comprehensive Manual Testing: Full-scale manual testing across different environments, ensuring all functionality is thoroughly tested and validated.
  • Salesforce Development & Customization: Develop Salesforce solutions, including Apex, LWC, flows, and nCino integrations.
  • Salesforce Integration Testing: Conduct manual integration testing to ensure smooth data flow between Salesforce and other systems.
  • Continuous Improvement & Support: Regularly review and improve Salesforce configurations and processes, ensuring everything stays optimized.
  • Full QA Support: Provide ongoing QA support, ensuring the stability and quality of all releases and fixes.
