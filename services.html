What I Offer

  • Salesforce Custom Configuration: I specialize in configuring **Salesforce objects**, **flows**, **CPQ**, and integrating third-party tools to meet your business needs.
  • Process Optimization: I help streamline workflows, improve release efficiency, and make your testing and deployment process faster and smoother.
  • Salesforce Integrations: I assist with integrating Salesforce with other CRM systems, API endpoints, and third-party services to extend its functionality.
